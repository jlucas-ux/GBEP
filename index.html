Category Definitions
NPS — Improve water quality through nonpoint source pollution abatement. PS — Improve water quality through point source pollution abatement. PHA — Promote public health and awareness. HC — Support habitat conservation. SC — Support species conservation. FWI — Sustain freshwater inflows. SPO — Preserve Galveston Bay through stakeholder and partner outreach. PEA — Support public education and awareness initiatives. RES — Collaborate with research institutions to support focus area applied research and monitoring. ACS — Increase access to Galveston Bay ecosystem information.
